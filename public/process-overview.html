Process Overview · SAGE × Workspace × AI

From blank Workspace to Award Setup Request — the end-to-end journey

A new tab — Workspace — joins SAGE's top nav, alongside Files. The Workspace replaces the old left-rail step flow: it is where the GM drafts and reconciles the budget. eGC1, Awards (NoA → Reconciliation → ASR), and Files all hang off the same top nav.

A The five stages, at a glance

1 Workspace draft
Workspace tab

Build the budget in blank Excel

GM, in the Workspace tab · pre-award

Workspace opens blank. Type rows directly, paste from past budgets, or click "AI prefill" to populate from similar past proposals. Each row uses real formulas — salary × effort × months, F&A on MTDC base — recalculating live.

✦ AI assist · Workspace
  • One-click prefill from 3 similar past proposals (sponsor, mechanism, dept)
  • Suggest a proposed total from past similar awards (median, avg, range)
  • Live UW Grad School + OPB rate lookup for salary and tuition
  • OCR linked invoices and propose row links
  • Pre-award mode: no NoA reconciliation UI — reconciliation is locked until Awards › NoA

The eGC1 Budget & Fiscal Compliance section auto-fills from Workspace rows, mapped to FAS Object Codes (01 Salaries, 04 Travel, 05 Supplies, 07 Benefits, 08 Student Aid). A "live preview" banner shows the form is mirroring Workspace; edits flow back via the "Edit in Workspace" link.

✦ AI assist · eGC1

  • Pre-answer Fiscal Compliance Q&A based on rate selection + cost sharing

3 Notice of Award

✦ AI assist · NoA

4 Reconciliation

Acknowledge & unlock workspace

Side-by-side cards: Workspace draft vs NoA awarded. PI notification, date impact (e.g., FY boundary crossing). Acknowledging flips Workspace into reconciliation mode — mismatch UI activates, NoA total becomes the new target.

✦ AI assist · Reconciliation

5 Award Setup Request
Awards tab · sub-stage 3

Map & route to GCA

GM submits · PI SFI gate

Mapping preview: every Workspace row → SAGE FAS object code. Pre-submission checklist gates submit on PI SFI. Final ASR routes Department › OSP › GCA.

✦ AI assist · ASR
  • Validate every line maps to a valid SAGE object code
  • Draft the routing comment with budget vs NoA reconciliation summary
  • Auto-nudge the PI for SFI when only that gate remains
Important design rule

Reconciliation is gated, not always-on

Pre-award the Workspace shows no NoA mismatches — it is a clean draft surface. The mismatch UI, red-row highlights, and "NoA target" reconciliation bar only appear after the GM explicitly opens Awards › Reconciliation and acknowledges the diff. This prevents the Workspace from feeling "broken" before an award even exists.

B Detailed flow with AI per stage

1

Workspace · blank Excel with formulas

The new starting point. Workspace tab is selected by default.

The GM lands on Workspace. Excel grid is blank — empty inputs for name, role, salary, effort, months. Sum row reads $0. The Proposed total field is empty; an AI button suggests a value from similar past proposals.

  • Formulas per row: personnel = salary × effort × months; fringe = rate × Σ personnel; tuition = per-quarter × students × quarters; F&A = rate × MTDC base.
  • Live recalc: change any input, all dependent subtotals + DC, F&A, total update.
  • Live data: UW Grad RA/TA salaries page and UW OPB Graduate Tuition Dashboard are linked from the right-panel Add-In.
  • No NoA UI yet: the reconciliation bar shows "Sum vs Proposed", no mismatches, no red rows.
✦ AI assist · Workspace
  • "AI prefill all rows" from 3 similar NIH R34 vision proposals
  • Proposed total suggestion: median + avg from past awards, click-to-accept
  • Per-row source chips (Workday salary, OPB rate, F&A agreement)
  • NIH Executive Level II cap warning on PI rows above the ceiling
  • OCR + invoice linking on Equipment row
2

eGC1 Forms · auto-populated

Single click from Workspace's floating dock.

Workspace rows flow into the eGC1 Budget & Fiscal Compliance section, aggregated into FAS object codes. The form pre-answers Fiscal Compliance questions and connects the SAGE Budget automatically.

  • Live preview — eGC1 mirrors Workspace until "Submit to Department" is clicked.
  • After submission, the form is locked but still visible.
✦ AI assist · eGC1
  • Map every Workspace row to its FAS code (01, 04, 05, 07, 08)
  • Pre-answer F-1/F-2/F-3 based on rate & cost-sharing inputs
  • Surface any value drift between Workspace formula and form
3

Awards · Notice of Award upload

When the sponsor's NoA PDF arrives.

Drag-and-drop into the Awards tab's first sub-stage. AI does structured extraction. The Reconciliation sub-tab is locked until upload completes.

✦ AI assist · NoA
  • 14 fields extracted with per-field confidence
  • Source chips back to PDF sections (§7, §I, §20, p.8)
  • Quick diff vs Workspace sum + total surplus/shortfall
4

Awards · Reconciliation

The gate that activates the reconciliation UI in Workspace.

Side-by-side amount + date deltas. PI notification CTA. Acknowledging this stage is the single switch that flips Workspace into reconciliation mode: the NoA total ($267,006) becomes the target, mismatch rows light up, the rounding-fix mismatch panel becomes available.

  • The ASR sub-tab is locked until reconciliation is acknowledged.
  • After acknowledgement, the GM is routed back to Workspace to revise.
✦ AI assist · Reconciliation
  • Draft a one-paragraph PI notification
  • Predict which rows will absorb the delta
  • Plain-English explanation of the diff (e.g., "6-month delay crosses July 1")
5

Awards · Award Setup Request (ASR)

Once Workspace matches NoA, ASR finalizes routing.

Every Workspace row mapped to a SAGE object code. Pre-submission checklist. Blocked on PI SFI; one button sends a reminder. On submit, ASR routes Department › OSP › GCA.

✦ AI assist · ASR
  • Validate & auto-suggest object codes for ambiguous rows
  • Generate the routing comment with budget vs NoA reconciliation summary
  • Auto-nudge PI for SFI; track completion

D Two-way sync — when SAGE Budget changes after import

The question

Stages 1 → 5 push Workspace → SAGE. After ASR submits, SAGE Budget becomes the system of record. If GCA edits SAGE (rounding fix, salary cap reduction, reallocation), what should the Workspace do?

Recommendation

Start with banner-only notifications; graduate to auto-pull with tracked changes for known-safe categories.

  • Auto-pull silently: F&A recalcs, fringe rate updates, salary cap reductions, tuition rate refreshes
  • Banner only: direct cost reallocations (GCA moved $5k from Travel to Supplies)
  • Conflict UI: personnel rows with unsaved Workspace edits

Why this works in the new structure

Tab-based nav makes the source of truth clear by context.

  • In the Workspace tab, Workspace is the source.
  • In the Budgets tab (SAGE Budget record), SAGE is the source.
  • The banner appears in Workspace when a SAGE-side change differs from Workspace; click "review" to open a diff.

E Open design questions

1. Multiple Workspaces per budget?

If a GM wants to compare two budget scenarios (best/worst case), should the Workspace tab support multiple drafts under one Budget ID?

2. Files tab and Workspace files

Files tab shows everything (NoA, invoices, exports, snapshots). Workspace has a paperclip dock for invoices linked to rows. Should the Workspace also have an inline "Files for this budget" preview, or is the Files tab enough?

3. When is the Workspace "closed"?

After ASR submit? After GCA finalizes? After project closeout? Each implies a different sync direction with SAGE Budget.

4. PI access vs GM access

Today the prototype assumes the GM. If the PI logs in, should they see a read-only Workspace view + the PI Review panel? Or only the eGC1 / Awards tabs?
